Formula Student

We are the University of Tasmania's Formula Student team. UTAS Motorsport is a student-led Formula SAE team from the University of Tasmania, bringing together students from engineering, business, design, and other disciplines to design and build a fully electric formula-style race car. Our team operates entirely through volunteer commitment, with students dedicating their time outside of university studies to gain practical industry experience, collaborate across disciplines, and represent Tasmania on a national and international stage.
