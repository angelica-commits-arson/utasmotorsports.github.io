Our Legacy

2001

The Beginning

First competition entry.

Establishment of the team and development of the first generation of Formula SAE-style vehicles. 6th overall out of 18 teams.
2002

Learning the Competition

New year, new car.

Keeping the 2001 chassis, the 2002 car was fitted with new internal parts based of judge’s criticisms.
2014

A Return

Team returns to competition after hiatus.

After a 12-year hiatus the team came 18th out of 26th but managed to achieve 3rd place for fuel efficiency and the CAMS ‘Inspiring Motorsport Award’.
2015

Team Expansion

Business team ranks 5th

Back in the midfield coming 15th out of 30 teams, however a dramatic improvement in static events placing 5th in Business presentation.
2016

Consistency

Continued development.

Consistent participation in the national Formula SAE competition, with year-on-year improvements in design and performance
2017

New Era

Fully electric

Our first Electric Vehicle went to competition with the team switching from combustion vehicles.
2018

Many Hands Make Light Work.

More subteams as the team grows in size.

Introduction of structured engineering workflows across mechanical, electrical, and aerodynamic systems.
2019

Community Campaigns

Building connections and a presence.

Strengthened presence at major Tasmanian motorsport events.
2022

Undermine the Competition

Returned to track

The UTAS team being only 1 of 6 teams to complete all dynamic events out of a total 20 teams.
2023

Revival

Back harder than ever.

4th overall and 3rd in the endurance event.
2024

Money Talks

Efficiency is key

A competitive return to racing, 2nd in efficiency and cost.
2025

Stronger than ever

What's my name?

Increased statewide presence by attending a broader range of community and motorsport events, significantly boosting public engagement and sponsor visibility. Built on the successful 2024 engineering methodologies to further streamline design, testing, and team coordination.

Championship Domain

Secured dominant title leads on track circuits.
