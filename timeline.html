Phase 01 // Q1

Stratum Shift

The layout breaks away from rigid vertical containers, implementing sweeping horizontal bands that establish a sense of geological pacing.

Phase 02 // Q2

Isolated Segments

By forcing generous negative space between the layout bands, elements breathe completely decoupled from one another. They never touch.

Phase 03 // Q3

Intersection Depth

As layers pass underneath content cards, drop shadows scale up to emphasize physical separation and structural parallax.

Phase 04 // Q4

Luminescent Destination

Approaching the foot of the experience reveals the focal bright spot, igniting elements visually as they scroll deep into the layout.
