About the Team

// Driven by student excellence

Welcome to our team. We are a collection of high-performance minds collaborating to design, build, manage, and showcase competitive racing infrastructure from the ground up.

The UTAS Motorsport Team was founded in 2001 with a clear purpose: to give students the opportunity to engage in practical, industry-aligned engineering and project work that extends far beyond the classroom. Over the years, the team has evolved into a highly collaborative, multidisciplinary organisation where students gain experience in design, manufacturing, business operations, leadership, and motorsport engineering.

Our team operates much like a professional motorsport outfit. Students work across specialised sub-teams—including mechanical design, electrical systems, aerodynamics, manufacturing, business, and operations—while contributing to the collective goal of producing a competitive Formula SAE vehicle. This structure ensures that every member, regardless of degree or background, plays a meaningful role in the team’s success.

What truly defines the team is its culture. We are a close-knit group of passionate individuals who share a strong commitment to innovation, teamwork, and continuous improvement. The team environment encourages mentorship, problem-solving, and personal growth, creating a supportive space where students can challenge themselves, develop new skills, and build confidence in their abilities. Beyond engineering, the UTAS Motorsport team fosters leadership, communication, and professional readiness. Students learn to manage deadlines, collaborate under pressure, and engage with industry professionals. These experiences prepare our members for future careers in engineering, manufacturing, motorsport, and related industries, making the team one of the most valuable student development programs at the University of Tasmania.
