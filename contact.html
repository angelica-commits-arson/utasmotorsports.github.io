Frequently Asked Questions

Who can join the team?

Our team is open to anyone currently studying at UTAS. As long as you have a passion for motorsports and can commit to a few hours a week, you're in.

How can new students join the team?

Recruitment opens at the start of each semester with more information and formal recruitment at C&S days. We welcome students from all degrees - not just engineers.

How do sponsorships work?

We partner with companies through tiered sponsorship levels offering brand exposure, talent recruitment access, and networking. Visit our Sponsors page or drop us a message.

What Events Can I attend with the Club

The team gets involved in various events across Tasmania. This includes Supercars, Targa Rally and Local car club events. Internally you can get involved in testing days and team building events

What if I want to join but don't know what to do?

Don't worry! The team provides plenty of different sub-groups. Interested in photography? Join our media team and expand your portfolio. Like planning events? Join our event team and plan unforgettable moments. Interested in building a fast car? Join one of our engineering subteams. Prefer to work on finance management skills? Our finance team is always searching for people.
